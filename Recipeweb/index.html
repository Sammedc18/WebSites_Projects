Veg Biryani Recipe

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 1 medium Onion, sliced
  • 1 medium Tomato, chopped
  • 2 Green Chillies, slit
  • 1 inch Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 1/4 cup Mint Leaves
  • 1/4 cup Coriander Leaves
  • 2 tbsp Ghee or Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Mixed Vegetables (Carrots, Peas, Cauliflower, Beans)
  • Water
  • Fresh Coriander for garnishing
  • Lemon wedges (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak Basmati rice in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a large pot.
  3. Add sliced onions and saute until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chillies. Saute for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  6. Add all the spices (turmeric, red chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, cardamom powder) and mix well.
  7. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  8. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot.
  9. Add enough water (usually 1.5 times the rice) and salt.
  10. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with raita or yogurt.
