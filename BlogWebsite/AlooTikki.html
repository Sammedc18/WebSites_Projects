Aloo Tikki Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4-5 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup boiled peas
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger (grated)
  • 1 tablespoon coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the mashed potatoes and boiled peas.
  2. Add the chopped onion, green chilies, ginger, and coriander leaves to the mixture.
  3. Season the mixture with cumin powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt.
  4. Mix everything together well, and divide the mixture into small portions.
  5. Shape each portion into a round or oval patty (tikki).
  6. Roll each tikki in breadcrumbs to coat evenly.
  7. Heat oil in a pan and fry the tikkis until they are golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  8. Serve hot with green chutney or tamarind chutney.
