Sev Puri Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6-8 Sev Puri (crispy puris)
  • 1/2 cup boiled potatoes (diced)
  • 1/4 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (whisked)
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind chutney
  • 2 tablespoons green chutney
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 cup sev (crispy gram flour noodles)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Place the sev puris on a serving plate.
  2. Top each puri with diced boiled potatoes and chickpeas.
  3. Pour whisked yogurt over the puris.
  4. Drizzle tamarind chutney and green chutney over the yogurt.
  5. Sprinkle chaat masala, cumin powder, red chili powder, and a pinch of salt.
  6. Top with sev for a crispy texture.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
