Pani Puri Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina
  • 1/2 cup wheat flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves
  • 2 green chilies
  • 2 tbsp tamarind pulp
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Mix semolina, wheat flour, baking soda, and water to form a dough. Rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Roll small balls and flatten them into puris. Fry until crispy and golden.
  3. Blend mint, coriander, green chilies, tamarind, and water to make the pani.
  4. Add salt and chaat masala to the pani. Adjust seasoning as needed.
  5. Serve the puris filled with spiced water and your choice of fillings.
